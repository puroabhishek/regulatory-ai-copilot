# Data handling and privacy policy

## 1. Purpose
This policy defines baseline controls and operating practices for **My Fintech** to meet applicable regulatory expectations.

## 2. Scope
Applies to relevant systems, data, vendors, and processes used by My Fintech.

## 3. Business Context (from Profile)
- **Business**: My Fintech
- **Country**: Qatar
- **Regulator**: QCB
- **Sector**: Government
- **Business type**: Insurance
- **Business model**: B2B2C
- **Target customers**: Consumers
